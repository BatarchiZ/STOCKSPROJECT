These data points will not be used for the stock portfolio, because the existence of extreme values, could be a signal of over-speculation or an unhealthy management type. Therefore, it was decided to filter these data points.

Method for filtering: I will be using my median and IQR data to identify boundaries and remove data points above and below the boundary. The formula to identify an outlier will be (Q1-1.5IQR or Q3+1.5IQR).
